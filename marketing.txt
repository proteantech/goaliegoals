Goalie is an application designed to help you create, manage, and achieve SMART goals. It begins by making sure your goals adhere to the SMART criteria (Specific, Measurable, Action-Focused, Results-Oriented, Time-bound). Once you've created your goals, you can log your progress and  quickly determine if you are on track to meet your goals. It also allows you to see how far ahead or behind you are on your goals. This kind of instant visual feedback is critical to helping you succeed in attaining your goals.
